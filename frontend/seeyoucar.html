Frequently Asked Questions

We accept most passenger vehicles including cars, trucks, SUVs, and motorcycles. We typically accept vehicles that are less than 15 years old with under 150,000 miles, though exceptions can be made for specialty or collector vehicles.

There is no upfront cost to list your vehicle. We charge a success fee of 5% of the final selling price, which is only collected if your vehicle sells. This fee covers our verification services, auction platform, and payment processing.

If your vehicle doesn't meet the reserve price during the auction, we can work with you to either adjust your expectations and relist, or we can extend our network offer program where we present the highest bids for your consideration without obligation.

Once the auction ends and the buyer's payment is verified, we typically process seller payments within 1-2 business days. Funds can be transferred directly to your bank account via ACH or wire transfer.
