Place Your Bids

Our transparent bidding system makes it easy to participate in auctions and compete for your dream car.

Step 1

Set Your Maximum Bid

Determine the highest amount you're willing to pay for the vehicle. Our system will automatically bid incrementally on your behalf up to your maximum amount.

Step 2

Monitor Auction Progress

Track the bidding activity in real-time. You'll receive notifications if you're outbid or when the auction is ending soon.

Step 3

Adjust Your Bid

You can increase your maximum bid at any time during the auction if you decide the vehicle is worth more to you.

Step 4

Final Countdown

In the last minutes of the auction, any new bid extends the auction time by two minutes to prevent last-second sniping and ensure everyone has a fair chance.

Understanding Reserve Prices

Some auctions have a reserve price set by the seller. If the bidding doesn't reach this minimum threshold, the seller isn't obligated to sell. Look for the "No Reserve" badge for auctions with no minimum price requirement.
